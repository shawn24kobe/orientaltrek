About Foshan Furniture

Leading manufacturer of premium oriental modern furniture since 2010

Our Story

Established in the heart of Foshan Shunde, China's furniture manufacturing capital, we have been crafting exceptional furniture that blends oriental aesthetics with modern design principles. With over a decade of experience, we have become a trusted partner for overseas buyers worldwide.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our state-of-the-art factory spans over 50,000 square meters with advanced production lines capable of handling both small custom orders and large-scale projects. We employ skilled artisans and utilize cutting-edge technology to ensure every piece meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Global Export Experience

We have successfully exported to over 80 countries, including major markets in North America, Europe, Australia, and the Middle East. Our furniture graces hotels, resorts, residential projects, and commercial spaces across the globe.

Design & Innovation

Our in-house design team combines traditional Chinese craftsmanship with contemporary trends to create timeless pieces. We offer OEM/ODM services and can customize furniture to meet specific design requirements and branding needs.

Quality Assurance

Quality control is at the core of everything we do. From raw material selection to final packaging, every step undergoes rigorous inspection. We adhere to international standards and provide comprehensive warranties on all our products.
